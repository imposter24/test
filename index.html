Our Mission

IndUS of Fox Valley, Inc. is dedicated to promoting Indo-American friendship and goodwill by serving the community through social, cultural, education and charitable activities. Our goals include organizing social and cultural programs which promote mutual awareness and understanding, conducting educational activities and events celebrating diversity, undertaking charitable work to assist and empower those in need, building a creative and dynamic organization that stays effective in achieving its mission, and supporting and collaborating in the activities undertaken by the organizations whose goals are in alignment with of IndUS.

Seva

Green Bay Seva started serving meals at the Community Shelter in Green Bay in March 2018. Seva, in Sanskrit, means service. It has a strength of 33 families so far. The group consists of all volunteers and 130-180 people has served each time. The group has also donated money to the Golden House and Green Bay Public Schools. The Seva is coordinated by Dr. Sabeena Kathuria, Radhika Rimmalapudi, Sridevi Buddi and Jayashree Murali.

Sandesh

Sandesh is the newsletter of IndUS of Fox Valley that was started in January 2003. Sandesh, in Sanskrit, means the message. Besides keeping the members and well-wishers informed about our activities and programs, IndUS has evolved into a journal addressing issues of special interest that affects the people in India and the USA. Issues have been dedicated to topics such as architecture, education, healthcare, social entrepreneurship, cultural identity, poetry, arts, music, and dance. We invite authors from both India and the US to share their expertise, experience, and perspectives. Sandesh welcomes articles and suggestions for future issues.
